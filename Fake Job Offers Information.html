Introduction


In the recent times, there have been a sudden increase in the Cybercrimes related to various online frauds and one of them being Fake job offers.

Most of the job seekers tend to fall prey to such Scamsters very easily and get deprived of their money in an attempt to get recruited to those FAKE Jobs.


Following is the list of possible Fake Job victims:

  1. Graduate students looking for fresh jobs.

  2. Professionals who want to switch their jobs for upskilling/ better packages.

  3. People who have interest for working in Foreign countries (I.T sector).

  4. Persons looking for some unorganized sector jobs like Electrician, Nurse, Plumber, Masons etc in the middle east.
